# ADR-0001: Stock FreeSWITCH

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-05-26

## Decision

Use stock FreeSWITCH.

Do not fork or replace it.

Integrate through supported extension interfaces such as `mod_xml_curl`, `ESL` / `mod_event_socket`, and minimal Lua helpers.
